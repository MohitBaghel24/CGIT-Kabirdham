Our Offerings

Departments & Courses

CGIT Kabirdham offers a robust selection of engineering disciplines and foundational studies, designed to equip students with the knowledge and skills for a successful career. Dive into our departments below.

Computer Science & Engineering

The Department of Computer Science & Engineering (CSE) is a dynamic hub for aspiring innovators. We delve deep into the core principles of computing, programming, data structures, algorithms, and software development. Our curriculum is constantly updated to reflect the latest industry trends, ensuring students are proficient in cutting-edge technologies like Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Cybersecurity, and Cloud Computing. With state-of-the-art labs and experienced faculty, we prepare students to be leaders in the digital world.

Courses Offered:
  • Diploma in Computer Science & Engineering
  • B.E. in Computer Science & Engineering (Affiliation to CSVTU, Bhilai)
Learn More

Electrical Engineering

The Electrical Engineering Department is where the future of power and energy takes shape. Our program covers fundamental concepts in circuit theory, power systems, control systems, and electronics. Students gain hands-on experience with electrical machines, power electronics, and industrial automation through well-equipped laboratories. We focus on developing problem-solving skills for real-world challenges in power generation, transmission, distribution, and sustainable energy solutions, preparing graduates for diverse roles in the energy sector.

Courses Offered:
  • Diploma in Electrical Engineering
  • B.E. in Electrical Engineering (Affiliation to CSVTU, Bhilai)
Learn More

Electronics & Telecommunication Engineering

The Electronics & Telecommunication (ET&T) Department offers a deep dive into the fascinating world of communication technologies and electronic systems. Our curriculum encompasses analog and digital electronics, microprocessors, communication systems, signal processing, and embedded systems. Students work on projects involving circuit design, mobile communication, optical fiber technology, and VLSI design. We foster innovation and provide the skills necessary to excel in telecommunications, consumer electronics, and IT industries.

Courses Offered:
  • Diploma in Electronics & Telecommunication Engineering
  • B.E. in Electronics & Telecommunication Engineering (Affiliation to CSVTU, Bhilai)
Learn More

Science & Humanity

The Department of Science & Humanity provides the foundational bedrock for all engineering disciplines. This department focuses on imparting essential knowledge in Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, and Communication Skills, which are crucial for a strong engineering education. Beyond technical subjects, it also instills ethical values, critical thinking, and holistic development through courses in humanities. This comprehensive approach ensures that our students are not just skilled engineers but also well-rounded individuals capable of contributing meaningfully to society.

Key Areas:
  • Applied Physics & Chemistry
  • Engineering Mathematics
  • Communication Skills & Professional Ethics
  • Environmental Science
