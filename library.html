Welcome to Our Library

The Heart of Academic Life at CGIT

Welcome to the central library of CGIT Kabirdham. More than just a collection of books, our library is a vibrant center for learning, research, and collaboration. We house an extensive collection of resources, including **over 40,000 books**, journals, and digital assets, all curated to support our rigorous B.Tech and Diploma programs. Our mission is to provide every student and faculty member with the information and support they need to excel in their academic pursuits.

Our Extensive Collection

Our library boasts a rich and diverse collection of over 40,000 volumes, covering all branches of engineering, applied sciences, and humanities. We provide a comprehensive range of textbooks, reference books, and technical manuals to directly support the CSVTU curriculum.

We also subscribe to a wide array of national and international journals, technical magazines, and periodicals, ensuring our students and faculty stay updated with the latest industry trends and research.

Modern Digital Library

Embracing the future of learning, our digital library provides access to a vast repository of online resources. Students and faculty can explore thousands of e-books, e-journals, and online databases from leading publishers and academic societies.

Equipped with high-speed internet and modern computer terminals, this section is designed to facilitate online research, access to digital courseware, and self-paced learning, providing a powerful supplement to our physical collection.

Dedicated Reading Room

Our spacious and well-ventilated reading room provides a silent, peaceful, and comfortable environment, ideal for focused study and deep concentration. With ample seating and proper lighting, it is the perfect place for students to dive into their coursework, prepare for exams, or simply get lost in a good book.

The reading room is designed to be a "no-disturbance" zone, fostering an atmosphere of academic discipline and scholarly pursuit for all members of our college community.

Our Staff

Our dedicated library staff are always available to assist you. From locating books and using digital resources to helping with research and referencing, our team is here to ensure you make the most of the library's facilities. Don't hesitate to ask for help!

General Rules
  • Maintain absolute silence in the reading room.
  • Mobile phones must be on silent mode.
  • No food or beverages are allowed inside.
  • Return borrowed books on or before the due date.
  • Handle all library property with care.
