Our Curriculum

Engineering Excellence (B.Tech & Diploma)

CGIT Kabirdham is proud to offer a comprehensive range of technical programs approved by AICTE and affiliated to CSVTU, Bhilai. We are excited to introduce three new cutting-edge B.Tech programs starting from the 2025-26 session, alongside our strong foundation of Diploma courses. Our curriculum is designed to be rigorous, practical, and industry-relevant, preparing students to become skilled engineers and innovators.

Degree (B.Tech) Programs

Intake: 60

B.Tech in Data Science

Harness the power of data. This program is designed to create experts in AI, Machine Learning, and Big Data analytics. You'll learn to analyze complex datasets, build predictive models, and drive decision-making in a world increasingly reliant on data. This is your chance to be at the forefront of the technology revolution.

Intake: 60

B.Tech in VLSI Design & Technology

Design the next generation of microchips. The VLSI industry is the backbone of all modern electronics. This course covers the design, fabrication, and testing of integrated circuits, preparing you for a high-demand career in the semiconductor industry. You will be engineering the technology that powers everything from smartphones to satellites.

Intake: 60

B.Tech in Power System Engineering

Power the nation's future. This specialized B.Tech program focuses on the generation, transmission, and distribution of electrical power. You will dive deep into smart grids, renewable energy sources, and power electronics, gaining the skills needed to manage and innovate in the critical energy sector.

Diploma Programs

Intake: 30

Diploma in Computer Science & Engg.

Build the digital world. This program provides a strong foundation in software development, networking, web technologies, and database management. It's the perfect launchpad for a career in IT, equipping you with the practical skills that industries demand.

Intake: 30

Diploma in Electronics & Telecommunication

Connect the world. This course covers the fundamentals of communication systems, electronic circuits, and wireless technology. Students learn to design, install, and maintain the systems that keep us connected, from mobile networks to fiber optics.

Intake: 30

Diploma in Electrical Engineering

Master the fundamentals of power. This core engineering discipline focuses on electrical machinery, power distribution, and control systems. You'll gain hands-on experience in our labs, preparing you for a robust career in the industrial and public sectors.

Intake: 60

Diploma in Costume Design & Dress Making

Unleash your creativity. This unique program blends artistic expression with technical skill, taking you into the dynamic world of fashion. Learn everything from garment construction and pattern making to textile science and fashion illustration, turning your creative concepts into runway-ready realities.
