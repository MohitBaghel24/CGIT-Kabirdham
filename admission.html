Admissions 2025-26

Your Path to an Engineering Career

Welcome to the admissions portal for CGIT Kabirdham. We are thrilled to launch our new B.Tech programs. All admissions for B.Tech (1st Year) and B.Tech (Lateral Entry) are processed through the centralized online counseling conducted by the **Directorate of Technical Education (DTE), Chhattisgarh**, based on your entrance exam merit scores.

Eligibility Criteria

B.Tech (1st Year)
  • Passed 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, & Maths (PCM).
  • Minimum 45% marks (40% for SC/ST/OBC).
  • A valid score in CG PET or JEE (Main).
B.Tech (Lateral Entry)
  • Passed 3-Year Engineering Diploma from a recognized Board/University.
  • Minimum 45% marks (40% for SC/ST/OBC).
  • Admissions are based on merit in the qualifying Diploma exam.

How to Apply

Your 4-Step Admission Process

1
Appear for Entrance Exam

Secure a valid merit rank in either the **CG PET** (conducted by CG Vyapam) or the **JEE (Main)** (conducted by NTA).

2
Register for Counseling

All seats are filled via the centralized online counseling by **DTE, Chhattisgarh**. You must register on their official portal.

3
Choice Filling & Allotment

During counseling, fill in your preferred colleges and branches. Based on your merit rank, a seat at CGIT Kabirdham may be allotted to you.

4
Report to CGIT Kabirdham

Once a seat is allotted, report to the institute on the specified date with all required original documents for verification and admission.

Entrance Exam Pathways

CG PET

The Chhattisgarh Pre-Engineering Test (CG PET) is the primary state-level entrance exam. A majority of seats in CGIT Kabirdham are filled based on the merit rank obtained in this examination, especially for candidates with a Chhattisgarh domicile.

JEE (Main)

The Joint Entrance Examination (JEE - Main), conducted by the NTA, is the national-level entrance exam. Seats are also reserved and filled based on the All India Rank (AIR) from JEE (Main), providing a pathway for students from all over India.

Documents Required

Please keep the following original documents (along with 2-3 sets of photocopies) ready for the counseling and admission process:

  • 10th Standard (High School) Marksheet
  • 12th Standard (HSC) Marksheet
  • CG PET or JEE (Main) Rank Card / Score Card
  • DTE Allotment Letter
  • Domicile Certificate (Niwas Praman Patra)
  • Caste Certificate (Jati Praman Patra), if applicable
  • Income Certificate (Aay Praman Patra), if applicable
  • Transfer Certificate (TC) from your last school
  • Character Certificate (CC) from your last school
  • Recent Passport Size Photographs (4-6 copies)
