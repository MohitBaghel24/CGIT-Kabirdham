About Us

Welcome to CGIT Kabirdham

(Approved by AICTE, New Delhi | Affiliated to CSVTU, Bhilai)

Chhattisgarh Institute of Technology, Kabirdham (CGIT Kabirdham) is a Premier Technical Institute, Upgraded by the Government of Chhattisgarh as part of its Commitment to Expanding High-Quality Engineering Education in Rural and Aspirational Districts. In line with Hon’ble Prime Minister Narendra Modi’s Vision for Strengthening Technical Education, CGIT Kabirdham has been Developed on an IIT-like Model to offer cutting-edge B.Tech and Diploma Programs from the 2025–26 Academic Session.
