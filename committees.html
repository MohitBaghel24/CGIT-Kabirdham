Our Framework

The Pillars of CGIT's Governance

At CGIT Kabirdham, a robust framework of committees underpins our commitment to academic excellence, student welfare, and transparent administration. These dedicated bodies comprise faculty members, administrative staff, and student representatives, working tirelessly to uphold standards, resolve issues, and foster a thriving campus community.

Key Roles of Our Committees:

  • Ensuring fairness and transparency
  • Promoting student and staff welfare
  • Maintaining discipline and safety
  • Facilitating academic and administrative efficiency
  • Resolving disputes and complaints promptly
  • Fostering an inclusive campus culture

Grievance Redressal Committee

The Grievance Redressal Committee is the cornerstone of fairness at CGIT. Dedicated to addressing complaints from students, faculty, and staff, this committee ensures that every voice is heard and every concern is treated with impartiality and respect. It acts as a trusted channel for resolving academic, administrative, or personal issues, fostering an environment where trust and equity prevail. Your peace of mind is our priority, and this committee works diligently to provide timely and just resolutions.

Internal Complaints Committee (ICC)

The Internal Complaints Committee (ICC) is paramount in creating a secure and respectful campus for everyone. Committed to preventing and addressing issues of sexual harassment, the ICC provides a confidential and supportive mechanism for reporting concerns. Members are trained to handle sensitive matters with empathy, discretion, and strict adherence to legal guidelines. This committee stands as a guardian of dignity, ensuring that every individual feels safe, valued, and empowered to pursue their academic and professional goals without fear.

Anti-Ragging Committee & Squad

CGIT Kabirdham is unequivocally committed to a ragging-free campus. Our Anti-Ragging Committee and Squad actively monitor the campus environment, swiftly investigate any reported incidents, and implement stringent measures to prevent ragging in all its forms. We believe in fostering a welcoming and nurturing atmosphere for new students, ensuring their transition into college life is positive and fear-free. This committee serves as a vigilant guardian, ensuring respect and camaraderie remain at the heart of our student interactions.

ST/SC Committee

The ST/SC Committee at CGIT is dedicated to promoting an inclusive and equitable educational environment for students belonging to Scheduled Tribes and Scheduled Castes. This committee works to address specific challenges, provide necessary support, and ensure that all policies and programs are implemented effectively to safeguard their interests. It acts as a vital support system, advocating for equal opportunities, facilitating access to resources, and fostering a campus culture where diversity is celebrated, and every student can achieve their full potential.

Purchase Committee

The Purchase Committee plays a crucial role in the efficient functioning of CGIT by overseeing all procurement processes. This committee ensures that all purchases, from laboratory equipment and library books to administrative supplies, adhere to strict standards of quality, cost-effectiveness, and transparency. With meticulous planning and ethical practices, they guarantee that the institute receives the best value for its investments, directly contributing to superior academic resources and operational efficiency.
